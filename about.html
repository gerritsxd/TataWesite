About Tata Steel

Our Heritage

Tata Steel is a leading global steel company with an annual crude steel capacity of 34 million tonnes. Established in 1907, it is one of the world's most geographically diversified steel producers. Our journey started over a century ago, driven by a vision to create a self-reliant India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build sustainable value for all stakeholders by delivering innovative steel solutions for tomorrow's challenges. We are committed to sustainable practices and community development, striving to make a positive impact on society and the environment.

Global Presence

With operations in over 26 countries and a commercial presence in over 50 countries, Tata Steel serves a wide range of markets, including automotive, construction, engineering, and packaging.
