About Tata Steel

Tata Steel is a leading global steel company with an annual crude steel capacity of 34 million tonnes. Established in 1907, it is one of the world's most geographically diversified steel producers, with operations and commercial presence across the world.

Our History

Founded by Jamsetji Tata, Tata Steel (formerly known as Tata Iron and Steel Company Limited) is the tenth largest steel producer in the world. The company was established in 1907 and has since grown to become a global leader in the steel industry.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build sustainable value for all stakeholders by delivering innovative steel solutions for tomorrow's challenges. We are committed to:

• Maintaining leadership in sustainable steel production
• Driving innovation in steel applications
• Creating value for our customers and society
• Ensuring environmental stewardship in all our operations

Sustainability Commitment

At Tata Steel, sustainability is at the core of our business strategy. We are committed to reducing our environmental footprint and contributing positively to the communities where we operate. Our sustainability initiatives focus on:

• Carbon reduction - Targeting carbon-neutral steel production by 2050
• Circular economy - Maximizing resource efficiency and minimizing waste
• Community development - Supporting education, healthcare, and livelihood projects

Innovation

Innovation is a key driver of our growth strategy. Our research and development teams work tirelessly to develop new steel grades, manufacturing processes, and applications that address emerging customer needs and societal challenges.
