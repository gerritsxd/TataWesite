About Us

Our Mission

We created this project to inspire change, because the youth now is our future. By encouraging young people to become civically engaged, we’re helping to shape a generation that isn’t afraid to speak up for one another and advocate for change where it’s needed. This includes standing up against injustice and fighting for the safety and livelihoods of everyone in the Netherlands. Our mission is to empower youth to become informed, active change-makers in society, and we believe that our site can help kickstart the movement. We are students from the University of Amsterdam (UvA) from the bachelor Computational Social Science (CSSci). We have collaborated with the Natuur en Milieu federatie to help make this project a reality. The project was also supervised by Dr. Steven Pickering, assistant professor in the Faculty of Social and Behavioural Sciences at the UvA.
