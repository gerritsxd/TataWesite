Organizations

Fridays for Future

Fridays for Future Netherlands is a youth movement fighting for the climate. They want the government and big companies to take real action against climate change now. They organize strikes and actions to make their message clear. You can support Fridays for Future, join their actions, or become a member to work together for a cleaner and better future.

Contact:

  • Email: info@fridaysforfuture.nederland
  • Instagram: fridaysforfutureamsterdam

Frisse Wind

Frisse Wind is a Dutch environmental group working for cleaner air and a healthier environment. They fight against air pollution and climate change by organizing actions, meetings, and campaigns. Everyone, including young people, can help out, support, or become a member to work together for a cleaner and better future.

Contact:

  • Website: frissewind.nu
  • Email: mail@frissewind.nu
  • Instagram: frissewindnu

Greenpeace

Greenpeace is an international group that fights to protect the environment and people’s health. In the Netherlands, they work to stop pollution from Tata Steel by organizing protests and campaigns. You can support or join Greenpeace to help make the air cleaner and protect nature.

Contact:

  • Website: greenpeace.nl
  • Email: info@greenpeace.nederland
  • Instagram: @greenpeacenederland

Gezondheid op 1

Gezondheid op 1 is an organization that stands up for the health of people living near Tata Steel. They fight for cleaner air and better protection against the harmful chemicals Tata Steel releases. They also participate in lawsuits to hold the factory accountable. You can support or join them to help create a healthier environment.

Contact:

  • Website: gezondheidop1.nl
  • Email: info@gezondheidop1.nl
  • Instagram: @gezondheidop1
